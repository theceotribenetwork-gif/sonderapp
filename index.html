How It Works

Five steps.
One real connection.

Every great connection on Sonder starts the same way — with who you actually are.

01

Build Your Profile

Record a short voice intro. Add your interests, favorite spots, and a text bio. No photo needed — not now, not ever unless you choose.

02

Get Matched on What Matters

Sonder surfaces connections based on shared interests, places you love, and compatibility — not how you look on a Tuesday afternoon.

03

Talk First

Connect via in-app voice and text chat. Hear their voice. Laugh with them. Everything stays inside Sonder — no numbers, no handles, no pressure.

04

The Reveal — On Your Terms

When you're both ready, send a Reveal Request. Only when both people say yes does a photo or video chat become available.

05

Define the Connection

Friends. More than friends. Something undefined. You choose — and if you're ready, plan your first IRL meetup right inside the app.
